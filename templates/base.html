About Us

Welcome to AT TRAVELS – your trusted travel companion, offering seamless and reliable bus booking solutions across India. At AT TRAVELS, our goal is to make travel accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Founded by Mr. Jason Antony M, AT TRAVELS was born out of a passion to transform the travel experience using smart, user-friendly technology. The company is steered with vision and leadership by our Chairperson,

Thank you for choosing AT TRAVELS – where every journey begins with care and trust.
